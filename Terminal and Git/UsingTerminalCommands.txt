Open Terminal

Navigate to your Desktop$ cd

Create a new directory, call it 'test'$ mkdir test

Make files called index.html, styles.css, command.txt inside the test directory$ cd test
$ touch index.html style.css command.txt

Make a cope of index.html and call it index_2.html$ cp index.html index_2.html

Navigate back to your desktop and make another directory, call it 'destination'$ cd
$mkdir destination

In the 'test' directory, remove the 'style.css' file$ rm style.css

Now, check and see what files are in the 'test' and 'destination' directory$ ls -la

Remove/delete the 'destination' directory$ rm -rf destination
